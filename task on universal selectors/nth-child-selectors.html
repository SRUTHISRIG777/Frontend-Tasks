cooking maggie:

Ingredients:

  • Maggie packets
  • water
  • salt
  • bataani
  • carrots
  • green chillies
  • aloo
  • oil

Making steps:

  1. switch on the stove
  2. place a pan on the stove and add a little oil.
  3. add chopped green chillies,carrots,aloo and bataani.
  4. fry the vegetable for 3 minutes
  5. pour the water into pan and add some salt.
  6. after water boiling, add the maggi noodles and masala.
  7. stir well and cook for about 3 minutes
  8. turn off the stove
  9. enjoy your maggie
